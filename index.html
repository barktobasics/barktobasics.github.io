About me

Hello, my name is Nancy! I am a Certified Animal Behaviourist (CAB) and graduated with my Diploma in Animal Behaviour (DipCABT) from COAPE in 2022. I currently offer training and behaviour consultations for both dogs and cats, and for problems ranging from house training and basic leash manners to reactivity and aggression.

My approach to training and behaviour modification uses reward-based and force-free techniques to build trust and teamwork between you and your animal. I have a passion for the role of choice and consent in treating fear-related behaviours such as reactivity and aggression, and using these to build an animal's confidence and ability to cope with the world they live in.

In addition to individual behaviour consultations, I help with training and behaviour at Vineyard Dog Agility, as well as offering group reactivity classes in Stellenbosch. I am a lecturer and tutor for COAPE International and play a role in keeping our courses updated with the latest science in animal behaviour. I also had the pleasure of contributing a chapter to Karin Pienaar's "Mood Matters", a book dedicated to the role of animal emotionality in the treatment of behaviour problems.

Frequently asked questions

If you are interested in help with training or behaviour, you can fill out my consultation form and I will get back to you with a suggested plan of action and fee. Depending on what you and your animal would like help with, this could involve a home visit, a private training class, a group reactivity class, or a full behavioural consultation.

Training focuses on teaching an animal to perform a particular exercise, such as sit, lie down, and stay, or more complicated behaviours such as running an agility course. Behaviour tends to refer to the more emotional side of things, such as aggressive behaviour that is driven by fear. In a behaviour consultation we focus on understanding and addressing the underlying emotions that drive a behaviour in order to achieve long-term success.

I work in Stellenbosch and the surrounding area. Please note that consultations outside of Stellenbosch may include an additional travel fee. I also offer online consultations and training via Zoom.
